Minced Meat

Home

Description

To prepare minced meat, cook chopped onions and garlic in oil until softened. Add minced meat and cook, breaking it apart, until browned. Season with spices like cumin and paprika, and add salt and pepper to taste. Optionally, add broth to simmer and bring the flavors together.

Serve the minced meat as a filling for tacos, wraps, or with rice, or use it in your favorite dish. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb minced meat (beef, chicken, or lamb)
  • 1 tablespoon oil (vegetable or olive oil)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup beef or chicken broth (optional)
  • Fresh herbs (parsley, cilantro) for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened.
  3. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute.
  4. Add the minced meat and cook, breaking it apart with a spoon, until browned.
  5. Stir in cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  6. Add the broth (optional) and simmer for a few minutes until the meat is cooked through and flavorful.
  7. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve hot!

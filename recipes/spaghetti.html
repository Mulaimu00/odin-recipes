Description

To make spaghetti, cook the pasta in boiling salted water until al dente. While the pasta is cooking, prepare your sauce by sautéing garlic and onions in olive oil, then adding tomatoes, herbs, and seasoning to taste. Let the sauce simmer until thickened.

Toss the cooked spaghetti with the sauce, and serve hot with grated cheese and fresh basil, if desired. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) crushed tomatoes (or fresh tomatoes)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and onion, sautéing until softened.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Once the pasta is done, drain it and toss with the sauce.
  5. Serve with fresh basil and grated Parmesan cheese, if desired. Enjoy!
